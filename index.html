About Me

I have a B.A. in Japanese language studies and East Asia studies with the main focus being on Japan. I also have a minor international business and economics. I decided to study software development in order to get into a stable career, where I could use my previous education and software programming together. I have always had a fascination with technology, and how it is programmed. Ideally, I would like to use software development at a company that has an environment where you can grow as a developer, and also be given chances to create programs that are beneficial to people. I would also like to work at a company that is either from Japan or interacts with Japan, so I can use my skills in Japanese language, and knowledge of Japanese culture and business practices. I have experience working with languages, so I know how important it is to keep on learning in order to improve your understanding of the language, which will make me a better developer that constantly is ready to learn new programming techniques or a new language. I also have done many team projects and presentations throughout my education, so I know how to work in teams to get projects done. I also know how businesses run, and the economic impact they can have in their own country and internationally. I have gained skills in customer service and time management at my previous jobs, so I understand how to treat clients, and get assignments done in a timely manner.
